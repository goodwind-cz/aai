## Verdict 2 — code_quality: PASS (0 BLOCKING, 1 NON-BLOCKING, 3 INFO)

Dispatch attention items, judged from the code:

1. **Injection surface of `git rev-parse --quiet --verify <tok>^{commit}`
   (docs-audit-core.mjs:233): none.** `tok` can only be a HASH_TOKEN_RE match —
   strictly `[0-9a-f]{7,40}` — so no shell metacharacter, whitespace, or option-shaped
   token (`-...`) can ever reach the execSync interpolation; `^{commit}` is inert in
   /bin/sh (POSIX sh performs no brace expansion) and the `^{commit}` peel plus `--quiet
   --verify` makes non-commit/ambiguous resolutions fail closed (git() → null → not
   counted — Article 4 degrade-to-suppression, never a flag). Attacker-shaped Evidence
   cells can at worst cause extra failed subprocesses.

   short-circuit), one subprocess per boundary-guarded hex token until first hit.
   Timestamp runs (`20260716T233008Z`) never tokenize. On this repo the full strict audit
